Kihon microframework v0.0.1

The framework

Motivation

Kihon( (基本) is born as the most current attempt to both develop applications faster and having a clean platform for it without spending ages dancing around mind-bogglingly stupid decisions other people took.

It is thus born from the spite and anger of having to rewrite bad code. It is born from the frustration of refactoring endlessly coupled and tangled code. It is born to simplify your and everybody else's life.

API

Kihon accomplishes that by getting the fuck out of your way and providing the following primitives and structure (click for details):

  • Web front controller
  • Console front controller
  • View/Controller primitives for the front controllers
  • Implementations for Singleton and Strategy
  • An URL - Controller mapper
  • A Configuration manager
  • A metric ton of documentation
  • A clear file structure
